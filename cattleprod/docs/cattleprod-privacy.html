Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, screens visited, and general interaction patterns to help improve the app.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique identifiers (e.g., advertising ID), and IP address.
  • Crash Reports: Stack traces and device state at the time of a crash.

How We Use Your Information

Advertising (AdMob):

  • To display ads, which may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user choice and region.

Analytics & Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics):

  • To diagnose problems, monitor stability, and improve the app.
  • Crash reports include device details, app version, and usage info immediately before a crash.

Data Sharing

  • Google AdMob: Receives data necessary to serve ads and perform analytics/fraud prevention.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google): Receives crash log data, used solely to help diagnose and resolve issues.

We do not sell or share your personal data with other third parties except as described above or as required by law.

Consent and Control

  • Advertising Choices: Users may opt out of personalized ads via device settings where available.
  • Crash Reporting: By using the app, crash logs will be transmitted automatically; to disable, stop using the app.
  • Children’s Privacy: The app is not targeted toward children under 13.

Data Retention

Ad and crash data are retained only as long as necessary to provide the service and meet legal obligations. Google’s retention policies also apply.

Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is fully secure.

Changes to This Policy
