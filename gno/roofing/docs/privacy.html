Privacy Policy

For the GNO Roofing lead qualification messaging service

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Mile Zero Enterprises, LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our GNO Roofing Lead Qualification SMS service (the “Service”). The Service is designed to respond to customer-initiated text messages about roofing issues, ask a few qualification questions, and—if you explicitly consent—connect you with a local roofing contractor.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Your phone number and any name you voluntarily provide.
  • Service Request Details: Information you submit such as ZIP code, general location, ownership status, and issue details.
  • Message Content: SMS messages exchanged with our automated assistant and related conversation metadata (timestamps, delivery status).
  • Technical/Operational Logs: System logs used to diagnose issues, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inbound request and ask qualification questions.
  • To determine whether your request fits participating contractor criteria.
  • To provide service-related updates during the qualification flow.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Service (including abuse prevention and troubleshooting).
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

SMS Consent and Controls

  • How you opt in: You opt in by texting our published business number first. We may ask you to confirm (e.g., “Reply YES to continue”).
  • Message types: Conversational, service-related messages (qualification questions, confirmation, and consent prompts).
  • Opt out: Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Rates: Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your conversation.

We do not send bulk promotional blasts or unsolicited marketing texts as part of this Service.

Data Sharing

  • Roofing Contractors (with consent): If you explicitly consent to be contacted, we may share your phone number and the details you provided with a local roofing contractor for follow-up.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party providers (e.g., SMS delivery, hosting) to operate the Service. These providers process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal / Safety: We may share information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the Service, maintain records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is fully secure.

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the Service after changes implies acceptance.
